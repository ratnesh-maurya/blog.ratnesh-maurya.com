## I can’t help rooting for tiny open source AI model maker Arcee
![Arcee CTO Lucas Atkins, CEO Mark McQuade](https://techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/01/Arcee-founders.jpg?w=1024)

**Arcee**, a lean US startup with just **26 employees**, has made a significant splash in the AI world by releasing its new reasoning model, **Trinity Large Thinking**. This model is a **400B-parameter** open-source Large Language Model (LLM), developed on a modest **$20 million** budget. CEO **Mark McQuade** proudly claims it is the "most capable open-weight model ever released by a non-Chinese company," highlighting its strategic importance.

The startup's ambition is to provide Western companies with a robust alternative to Chinese-based models, which are often perceived as carrying geopolitical risks, potentially compromising data or power. By offering an open-source model, **Arcee** allows companies to download and integrate the technology, ensuring greater control and addressing concerns about data sovereignty and alignment with Western ideals.

> **Arcee's** Trinity Large Thinking model, built on a **$20 million** budget by a **26-person** team, aims to provide Western companies with a high-capability, open-source alternative to Chinese LLMs, addressing perceived risks in data and governance.

## Anthropic Teams Up With Its Rivals to Keep AI From Hacking Everything
![Anthropic Teams Up With Its Rivals to Keep AI From Hacking Everything](https://media.wired.com/photos/69d4e951d8b67218cd4b81ad/master/w_2560%2Cc_limit/04072026_New%2520Anthropic%2520Industry%2520Coalition%2520Racing%2520to%2520Use%2520Private%2520Claude%2520Mythos%2520Model%2520To%2520Secure%2520Software.jpg)

**Anthropic** has formally announced **Mythos Preview**, a powerful new **Claude** model, alongside the launch of Project Glasswing, an industry consortium dedicated to addressing the cybersecurity implications of advanced AI. This initiative brings together over **45 organizations**, including major players like **Microsoft**, **Apple**, **Google**, **Amazon Web Services**, the **Linux Foundation**, **Cisco**, and **Nvidia**.

The consortium's primary goal is to leverage **Mythos Preview** to test and enhance AI cybersecurity capabilities. Members will have private access to the model to identify and mitigate vulnerabilities within their own systems, preparing for a future where AI capabilities could significantly disrupt current software security practices. **Logan Graham**, **Anthropic's** frontier red team lead, emphasized that the effort is about preparing the industry for a future where such advanced AI capabilities are broadly available, potentially breaking existing security paradigms within the next **6 to 24 months**.

> **Anthropic's** Project Glasswing unites over **45 tech giants** to proactively counter AI's potential to exploit software vulnerabilities, using the **Claude Mythos Preview** model to stress-test and fortify global cybersecurity defenses.

## Project Glasswing: Securing critical software for the AI era
![Project Glasswing: Securing critical software for the AI era - Anthropic](https://www.anthropic.com/_next/image?url=https%3A%2F%2Fwww-cdn.anthropic.com%2Fimages%2F4zrzovbb%2Fwebsite%2F337ec3073e807548e9254096b073de0df47ed6dc-250x250.png&w=256&q=75)

**Anthropic** officially announced Project Glasswing, a crucial initiative aimed at securing critical software against advanced AI threats. This project stems from the observed capabilities of **Anthropic's** new, unreleased frontier model, **Claude Mythos Preview**, which has demonstrated an ability to surpass most human experts in finding and exploiting software vulnerabilities. The model has already uncovered thousands of high-severity flaws, including issues in **every major operating system and web browser**.

Project Glasswing brings together a formidable list of launch partners, including **Amazon Web Services**, **Anthropic**, **Apple**, **Broadcom**, **Cisco**, **CrowdStrike**, **Google**, **JPMorganChase**, the **Linux Foundation**, **Microsoft**, **NVIDIA**, and **Palo Alto Networks**. These organizations will use **Mythos Preview** for defensive security work, with **Anthropic** sharing insights to benefit the wider industry. The company is committing up to **$100 million** in usage credits for **Mythos Preview** and **$4 million** in direct donations to open-source security organizations, underscoring the urgency of collaborative defense in the AI era.

> Project Glasswing is a critical multi-organizational effort, spearheaded by **Anthropic's Mythos Preview** model, to proactively address the severe cybersecurity risks posed by advanced AI capable of autonomously finding and exploiting vulnerabilities in **major operating systems and web browsers**.

## Adobe launches Acrobat Spaces, a free AI-powered study tool for students
![The Adobe Inc. logo displayed on a smartphone screen.](https://techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2024/09/GettyImages-2162453288.jpg?w=1024)

**Adobe** is expanding its AI capabilities beyond professionals to serve the student community with the launch of **Acrobat Spaces**. This new, free **AI-powered** study tool is designed to transform study materials such as PDFs, links, and notes into engaging learning aids like presentations, flashcards, and quizzes.

**Acrobat Spaces** aims to compete with other AI-driven educational platforms, including **Google’s NotebookLM**, **Goodnotes**, and **Turbo AI**. By offering this tool, **Adobe** seeks to make **Acrobat** more versatile and accessible for students, enhancing their ability to create customized study content from diverse sources. This move underscores **Adobe's** commitment to integrating AI across its product suite, catering to a broader user base and their evolving needs.

> **Adobe** has launched **Acrobat Spaces**, a free **AI-powered** tool for students that converts various study materials into presentations, flashcards, and quizzes, marking a strategic entry into the educational AI market to compete with established platforms.

## Google has launched a free, offline AI dictation app that will automatically polish your speech.
![Screenshots of Google AI Edge Eloquent as seen on the App Store](https://platform.theverge.com/wp-content/uploads/sites/2/2026/04/google-ai-edge-eloquent-app-store.jpg?quality=90&strip=all&crop=0%2C0%2C100%2C100&w=2400)

**Google** has introduced **AI Edge Eloquent**, a new live **AI** transcription app that operates offline, is completely free, and comes with no usage limits. Currently available on **iOS**, **Google** has confirmed plans to extend its availability to **Android** and **macOS** platforms.

One of the standout features of **AI Edge Eloquent** is its ability to automatically polish speech. After a user finishes speaking, the app intelligently filters out common filler words such as "um," resulting in a cleaner and more professional transcription. This innovative tool promises to be invaluable for anyone needing clear, concise dictation, from students to professionals, enhancing productivity without the need for an internet connection or subscription.

> **Google's** new **AI Edge Eloquent** app offers free, offline **AI** dictation on **iOS** that automatically refines speech by removing filler words, with future expansion planned for **Android** and **macOS**.
