## OpenAI Abruptly Drops AI Video Tool Sora, Blindsiding Disney
![OpenAI drops AI video tool Sora - iTnews](https://i.nextmedia.com.au/News/2026-03-24T195449Z_1_LYNXMPEM2N18Z_RTROPTP_4_OPENAI-SORA.JPG)
In a surprising turn of events, **OpenAI** has announced the immediate discontinuation of its much-hyped **AI** video generation tool, **Sora**. The decision came swiftly, reportedly blindsiding **Walt Disney** teams just **30 minutes** after a joint meeting regarding a project linked to **Sora**. This move marks a significant strategic shift for the **ChatGPT** maker, as it reorients its business focus towards potentially more lucrative areas such as coding tools and corporate customers.

This abrupt cancellation also signifies the end of a blockbuster **US$1 billion ($1.44 billion)**, **three-year deal** between **Disney** and **OpenAI** that had only been announced a little over **three months** ago. Under the agreement, **Disney** was to invest **US$1 billion** and contribute over **200** of its iconic characters for use in **AI**-generated videos. However, sources familiar with the matter confirmed that the transaction never formally closed, and no money changed hands.
