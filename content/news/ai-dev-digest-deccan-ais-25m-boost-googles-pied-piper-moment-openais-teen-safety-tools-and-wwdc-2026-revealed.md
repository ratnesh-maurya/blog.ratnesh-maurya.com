## Mercor competitor Deccan AI raises $25M, sources experts from India
![Deccan AI founder Rukesh Reddy](https://techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/deccan-ai-founder-rukesh-reddy.jpg?w=1024)

**Deccan AI**, a startup specializing in post-training data and evaluation for AI models, has successfully secured **$25 million** in its Series A funding round. This all-equity investment was led by **A91 Partners**, with additional participation from **Susquehanna International Group** and **Prosus Ventures**. The company is notable for relying significantly on an **India-based** workforce of experts to conduct much of its specialized work.

Founded in **October 2024**, **Deccan AI** addresses the growing demand for refining and making AI systems reliable for real-world applications. Its services span from enhancing coding and agent capabilities in models to training systems for interaction with external tools like APIs. The startup also works with frontier AI labs such as **Google DeepMind** and **Snowflake** to provide expert feedback, run evaluations, and build reinforcement learning environments.
