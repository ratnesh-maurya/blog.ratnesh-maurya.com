## Mistral is rumored to be raising €3B at €20B valuation
![The Mistral AI logo appears on a smartphone screen in this photo illustration](https://techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/03/GettyImages-2264771189.jpg?w=1024)
French AI lab **Mistral AI** is reportedly in early discussions to secure approximately **€3 billion** ($3.5 billion) in funding. This significant round would project the company's valuation to reach around **€20 billion** (about $23.15 billion), a substantial leap from its previous **€11.7 billion** valuation just last September.

**Mistral AI**, established in **2023**, has quickly emerged as a frontrunner among Europe’s AI startups. The company is distinguished by its more open stance on AI development, providing some foundational large language models with open weights, allowing for broader customization. Alongside these open models, **Mistral** also offers specialized closed models for applications like programming, voice cloning, and optical character recognition.

In a strategic move to cater to Europe's increasing desire for technological independence, **Mistral** has positioned itself as a "sovereign" and homegrown alternative to American tech giants. The company is actively setting up a data center near Paris and has forged partnerships to bolster its European presence.

> The potential **€20 billion** valuation underscores **Mistral AI's** rapid ascent and its strategic importance in the global AI landscape, particularly within Europe.

## Cheaper, faster, and culturally aware, Avataar’s video AI is built for India’s scale
![screenshot of Avataar AI characters](https://techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2026/06/Screenshot-2026-06-11-at-3.39.00-PM.jpg?w=1024)
India's AI development, while lagging behind the U.S., Europe, and China, is receiving a significant boost from initiatives like the **India AI Mission**, a **$1.2 billion** government program. This initiative aims to spur innovation by providing subsidized GPU compute access to selected startups in exchange for public model releases. Among the **12 startups** chosen, **Avataar AI** has launched a groundbreaking video model named **Varya**.

**Varya** is specifically designed with a deep understanding of local context, capable of identifying unique Indian festivals, foods, and clothing. Instead of building from scratch, **Avataar AI**, backed by **Peak XV**, utilized a technique called distillation, starting with **Alibaba's** publicly available **Wan 2.2** model. This process compressed **Wan 2.2's** capabilities into a leaner, faster version, optimizing it for **Avataar's** e-commerce video tools.

The result is a highly efficient model that executes in just **four steps** compared to **Wan 2.2's 50**, enabling **Varya** to generate video **10 times faster** and at a fraction of the cost. For example, using an **NVIDIA H200 GPU**, **Varya** can produce a **5-second 720p clip in 45 seconds**, a dramatic improvement over the **1,230 seconds** it would otherwise take.

> **Avataar AI's Varya** model represents a significant leap in localized AI, making culturally relevant video generation accessible and efficient for the Indian market at an unprecedented scale.

## Apple’s Camera Chief Thinks AI Can Give You Superpowers
![Image may contain Electronics Mobile Phone Phone and Iphone](https://media.wired.com/photos/6a2991665cc686c56079e1fd/master/w_1600%2Cc_limit/Siri%2520AI%2520in%2520Camera%2520SOURCE%2520Julian%2520Chokkattu.jpg)
**Apple** is introducing new generative AI features to its **Photos app** in **iOS 27**, set to arrive on iPhones later this year. While other tech giants like **Google** and **Samsung** have already integrated advanced generative AI for photo manipulation, **Apple's** iPhone camera chief, **Jon McCormack**, emphasizes a more deliberate approach, stating they are not "doing AI for the sake of AI."

At its annual **Worldwide Developer Conference (WWDC 2026)**, **Apple** showcased features like an enhanced **Clean Up** tool for erasing unwanted objects. More notably, new features called **Extend** and **Spatial Reframe** will allow users to expand photo backgrounds or alter image perspectives by generating "fake pixels." This capability enables the camera to intelligently infer and draw in what should be there, empowering users with advanced editing previously requiring professional software.

**McCormack** highlights that AI is helping to solve a "giant backlog of unsolvable issues," giving "normal people these absolute superpowers" without needing expert knowledge in tools like Photoshop. **Apple's** strategy focuses on practical, deliberate AI applications within the Photos app, though the article notes that more radical generative AI capabilities might be available through the App Store.

> **Apple's** new **iOS 27** AI features in the Photos app aim to democratize advanced image manipulation, providing users with "superpowers" through intelligent, generative pixel creation rather than simply adding AI as a trend.

## Meta’s Edits app is getting an AI assistant and a desktop version
![Meta’s Edits app is getting an AI assistant and a desktop version - TechCrunch](https://techcrunch.com/wp-content/uploads/2025/05/meta-edits.png?w=1024)
**Meta** recently previewed significant updates to its video editing app, **Edits**, at an invite-only creator event in L.A. These upcoming additions include a new **AI assistant** and a much-anticipated **desktop version**, moving beyond its initial mobile-only format. Launched last year as a direct rival to **ByteDance’s CapCut**, **Meta** is enhancing **Edits** to retain and attract more users in the competitive short-form video landscape.

The forthcoming **AI assistant** is designed to provide creators with valuable insights and content ideas by analyzing their **Instagram** data, such as views and video retention rates. It will help creators understand what content resonates with their audience and suggest new video concepts, including leveraging trending audio. This integration aims to streamline the creative process, reducing the need for creators to use external tools for analysis and brainstorming.

By offering an **AI assistant** directly within **Edits** and expanding to a desktop platform, **Meta** seeks to keep creators engaged within its ecosystem, thereby encouraging more frequent posting and boosting overall user engagement. This move is part of **Meta's** broader strategy to compete effectively with platforms like **TikTok** and **YouTube** for creator attention and content.

> **Meta's** integration of an **AI assistant** and a **desktop version** into its **Edits app** is a strategic move to empower creators with data-driven content ideas and a more flexible workflow, strengthening its position against rivals like **CapCut** and **TikTok**.

## I'm Glad Apple Isn't Hyping Up Agentic AI (Yet)
![Apple's Siri logo in front of Google's Gemini logo.](https://www.engadget.com/img/gallery/im-glad-apple-isnt-hyping-up-agentic-ai-yet/intro-1781185410.jpg)
While "agentic AI" has been the latest buzzword dominating recent tech keynotes from **Google I/O**, **Microsoft Build**, and **NVIDIA's Computex**, **Apple** has taken a decidedly different and less hyped approach. Agentic AI refers to intelligent agents capable of performing tasks autonomously, such as scheduling meetings from emails. However, concerns about the reliability of current AI models, which can still "hallucinate" or be untrustworthy, raise questions about the wisdom of fully autonomous agents at this stage.

During its **WWDC 2026** keynote, **Apple** only briefly touched upon agentic AI. Instead, the company focused extensively on demonstrating how its **new Siri AI** could offer practical utility to users. Examples included finding specific information within lengthy text message threads or assisting with securing tickets for exclusive concerts. **Siri AI** primarily responds to user commands, but leverages modern AI models for improved data synthesis and responsiveness.

Early observations of **Siri AI** at **WWDC 2026** suggest it performs as advertised, though long-term testing will be crucial to assess its sustained reliability. This measured approach, combined with **Apple's** commitment to privacy through **Private Cloud Compute**, positions it distinctly from competitors. The article notes an intrigue in **Siri AI's** possibilities, even for an AI skeptic, more so than for other offerings like **Microsoft's Copilot**.

> **Apple's** pragmatic focus on making **Siri AI** genuinely useful through enhanced data synthesis and user-commanded actions, rather than aggressively hyping agentic AI, sets it apart from industry trends and addresses concerns about AI reliability.
